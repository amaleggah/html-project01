Frida Kahlo's Legacy

Frida Kahlo broke down the barriers of femininity. Unbothered by the beauty standards of the time, she refused to shave her mustache and groom her unibrow. Furthermore, after suffering a brutal automobile accident, Frida Kahlo wore a prosthetic leg.

Frida Kahlo is an important feminist icon because she was not just another white woman. She embraced her sexuality as a queer woman; she proudly discussed her communist beliefs. Frida Kahlo challenges the notions of traditional white feminism.
